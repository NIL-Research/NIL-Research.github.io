Dr. Gleb Vzorin is an ongoing postdoctoral researcher at the Neurocognitive Image Lab, Shanghai International Studies University (SISU), and a research scientist at the Institute of Psychology, Russian Academy of Sciences. He earned his PhD at the Russian Academy of Sciences under the supervision of Prof. D. Ushakov, focusing on the impact of digital technologies on human memory. His earlier research at Lomonosov Moscow State University explored memory consolidation mechanisms under the influence of pharmacological interventions. His current research interests span human-machine co-evolution, the methodology of cognitive science, and neuroaesthetics.
