Jörg Fingerhut is a philosopher and experimental researcher at Humboldt University. His work sits at the intersection of philosophy, cognitive science, and the arts, with a focus on aesthetic experience, emotion, and culture. Through the Arts and Minds Lab and related collaborations, he studies how art and architecture can evoke wonder and transform understanding.
