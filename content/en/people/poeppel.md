Professor Ernst Pöppel is a world-renowned psychologist and neuroscientist. He has been elected as a member of multiple prestigious academies, including the German National Academy of Sciences, the European Academy of Sciences and Arts, the Academia Europaea, and the Russian Academy of Education.

His research focuses primarily on cognitive neuroscience, spanning psychology, neuropsychology, and sensory physiology, with particular emphasis on visual perception, temporal processing, and neuroaesthetics. His work reflects a philosophy of vertical and horizontal interdisciplinarity, connecting electrophysiology, behavioral studies, neuroimaging, rehabilitation, anesthesiology, neuroaesthetics, and artificial intelligence.
