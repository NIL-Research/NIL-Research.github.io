Beata Bajno is a visual artist and architect based in Warsaw. Her practice focuses on light, space, perception, and multimedia installation, exploring how people interact with different forms of light and how spatial experience shapes perception. Her work connects artistic practice with research on art perception and has been presented through exhibitions, publications, awards, and interdisciplinary collaborations.
