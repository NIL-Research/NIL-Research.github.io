Jacek Rogala is a neurobiologist working across neuroinformatics, cognitive and behavioral neuroscience, and neuroaesthetics. His research uses methods including EEG, fMRI neurofeedback, machine learning, and AI-based analysis of brain data. In neuroaesthetics, he examines how artistic intent may be encoded, transmitted, and perceived by the brain, and how the arts, humanities, and sciences can share a more precise vocabulary.
