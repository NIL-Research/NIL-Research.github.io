Alexander Benz is a lawyer associated with LMU Munich. His doctoral research at LMU focuses on the Applied Knowing approach to innovative learning. He has taught at the Institute for Medical Psychology and contributed to medical psychology teaching, communication and ethics curricula, faculty training, and change-management processes in medical education. His work links legal and educational experience with medical psychology, neuroethics, and neuroaesthetics.
