Helmut Leder is Professor at the University of Vienna, Faculty of Psychology, Department of Psychology of Cognition, Emotion and Methods. He has served as Head of Department and as Head of the Cognitive Science Research Hub, and was President of the International Association of Empirical Aesthetics from 2014 to 2018. His research focuses on empirical aesthetics, the psychology of aesthetics, art perception, and cognitive science.
