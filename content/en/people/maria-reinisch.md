Maria Reinisch is Managing Director of VDW in Germany. Her background combines economics, top management, and doctoral research in human biology on leadership perception. Since 2016, she has worked with VDW on critical and responsible science, addressing topics such as climate, renewable energy, biodiversity, smart cities, and peace, and translating scientific issues into creative public formats.
