Nikos Logothetis is Director of the International Center for Primate Brain Research in Shanghai and Emeritus Director at the Max Planck Institute for Biological Cybernetics. His work investigates the physiological mechanisms of visual cognition, auditory perception, multisensory integration, neural plasticity, and neuromodulation. He is known for combining multimodal approaches to study brain systems across scales.
