  NIL welcomes undergraduate students who are interested in research on cognition, image, aesthetics, artificial intelligence, and human-computer interaction.

  Undergraduate students may participate in research activities such as literature review, experimental material preparation, behavioral and eye-tracking experiments, data collection, basic programming, data analysis, and academic writing training. Students may also develop undergraduate research projects under supervision, depending on their interests, background, and project availability.

  ## Who Should Apply

  We welcome undergraduate students who:

  - Are interested in cognitive neuroscience, psychology, image research, AI, aesthetics, or human-computer interaction
  - Are willing to learn research methods and participate in long-term research training
  - Have good English reading ability
  - Have basic programming, statistics, design, or experimental experience, if available
  - Are responsible, self-motivated, and able to work in a team

  ## Possible Training Areas

  - Literature reading and research discussion
  - Experimental design and stimulus preparation
  - Eye-tracking and behavioral data collection
  - Data coding and analysis
  - Programming for experiments
  - Academic writing and presentation
  - Undergraduate research project development

  ## Contact

  Interested students may send a short CV and a brief statement of interest to [nil@shisu.edu.cn](mailto:nil@shisu.edu.cn).

  Suggested email subject: Name + Undergraduate Research + Major / Year.
