  NIL welcomes visiting students, joint-training students, exchange students, and international students who wish to participate in short-term or medium-term research activities.

  Visiting students may join ongoing projects related to cognitive neuroscience, image science, neuroaesthetics, artificial intelligence, human-computer interaction, eye tracking, behavioral experiments, or cross-cultural studies. The specific research plan and visiting period will be discussed based on the student's background, interests, and available supervision.

  ## Visiting Categories

  - Visiting students from domestic or international universities
  - Joint-training students
  - Exchange students
  - International students interested in NIL's research areas
  - Students seeking short-term research experience

  ## Expectations

  Visiting students are expected to:

  - Have a clear research interest related to NIL's research directions
  - Participate actively in research discussions and project activities
  - Follow research ethics and laboratory procedures
  - Maintain regular communication with supervisors or project mentors
  - Complete agreed research tasks during the visit

  ## Application Materials

  Interested students may send:

  - Curriculum vitae
  - Brief research interest statement
  - Proposed visiting period
  - Current institution and program information
  - Name of current supervisor, if applicable

  ## Contact

  Please send inquiries to [nil@shisu.edu.cn](mailto:nil@shisu.edu.cn).

  Suggested email subject: Name + Visiting Student + Proposed Visiting Period.
