## Overview

The 2024 Summer School, **"The Power of Aesthetics,"** was held from **August 24 to 31, 2024** at the Songjiang Campus of Shanghai International Studies University. Jointly hosted by Shanghai International Studies University, Ludwig Maximilian University of Munich, and the German Scientists Association, the program brought together faculty members and participants from different countries, generations, and academic fields for an intensive interdisciplinary exchange.

The Summer School focused on **"The Power of Aesthetics"** as a shared framework for exploring human knowledge, cognition, creativity, and modes of expression. Taking inspiration from interdisciplinary discussions on explicit knowledge, implicit knowledge, and visual knowledge, the program invited participants to reconsider aesthetics not only as a field of art or perception, but also as a broader framework for understanding human cognition and cultural production.

Experts from brain science, psychology, image science, art history, linguistics, physics, economics, management, neuroaesthetics, mathematics, musicology, and related fields joined the program. Through lectures, group discussions, short presentations, cultural visits, and collaborative project design, the Summer School created a setting for cross-disciplinary and cross-cultural academic dialogue.

## Organizers

The Summer School was jointly hosted by:

- Shanghai International Studies University (SISU)
- Ludwig Maximilian University of Munich (LMU)
- German Scientists Association / Federation of German Scientists (VDW)
- World Arthistory Institute (WAI), SISU

## Program Snapshot

The 2024 program included:

- Opening reception and participant orientation
- Culture Day activities in Shanghai
- Faculty lectures on aesthetics, cognition, art, time, image, and knowledge
- Short presentations by participants
- Group discussions and collaborative project design
- Interdisciplinary exchange among students, researchers, artists, and faculty members

## Faculty and Academic Themes

The Summer School gathered scholars and practitioners from multiple fields, including cognitive science, behavioral physiology, art history, psychology, linguistics and cognitive sciences, music composition, mathematics and art, physics and art, economics and development, leadership and ecology, cultural management, decision-making, and brain science.

## Highlights

- The Summer School developed an interdisciplinary dialogue around the central theme **"The Power of Aesthetics."**
- The program connected aesthetics with cognitive science, art history, image studies, psychology, linguistics, mathematics, music, and brain science.
- Participants engaged in lectures, short presentations, group discussions, and collaborative project design.
- Cultural activities in Shanghai provided an additional setting for reflection on visual knowledge, art, and aesthetic experience.
- The event helped establish a continuing Summer School series at SISU around aesthetics, cognition, and interdisciplinary research.
